Types of Software I Have Developed

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) development focuses on creating energy-efficient wireless communication between devices. It is widely used in IoT, healthcare, fitness trackers, and smart home applications. BLE allows devices to connect with low power consumption, making it ideal for battery-operated gadgets. Developers use BLE APIs to scan, connect, and exchange data between mobile apps and BLE-enabled hardware. Its lightweight communication makes it perfect for real-time sensor data transfer and remote monitoring.

VOIP

A VoIP application is a software application installed on a computer or smartphone that lets you make and receive telephone calls. Chances are you’ve already used one before. Popular personal VoIP apps include Skype, Viber, and WhatsApp.

Educational

Developing educational apps and learning software are exceedingly becoming popular among the global education community. They not only eliminate the hassle of carrying heavy books but also make learning more fun, interactive, and super engaging.

E-Commerce

Ecommerce is buying and selling that is conducted in an online environment, through an ecommerce website. An application is a software program designed to accomplish one or more tasks in a particular software environment.

Medical

Medical apps are software on a mobile device that function as an accessory to a regulated medical device or transform a mobile platform into a regulated medical device. These apps can help patients manage their own health and help healthcare practitioners facilitate and improve patient care.
